Our Story

A Passion for the Sea

Tucked inside Pazari i Ri, Tirana's most vibrant market district, Villa 78 is where the freshest catch of the day meets refined Mediterranean culinary tradition. The unmistakable aroma from our kitchen has drawn guests in from the street since day one.

From hand-picked oysters and wood-grilled seabass to our celebrated Risotto ai Frutti di Mare, every dish is a testament to quality sourcing, attentive preparation, and the kind of warm Albanian hospitality that transforms a meal into a memory.
